Proven in Deadlock
Brands Are Already Here

The Deadlock competitive scene has already attracted major brand investment, proving audience quality, community passion, and sponsorship viability before mainstream awareness hits.

Proven Precedent
Trolli × Deadlock Night Shift
Trolli USA sponsored a $20,000 combined prize pool across NA and EU tournaments organized by Deadlock Night Shift, the premier Deadlock production org. The partnership went beyond traditional sponsorship: DNS created custom in-game branded assets, a downloadable Trolli midboss mod that replaced in-game characters with Trolli branding, visible on every stream, in every clip, permanently in VODs. A candy brand found meaningful ROI in competitive Deadlock. A global peripherals leader has an even stronger case.

If Trolli can fund a $20K Deadlock tournament and create branded in-game assets, Logitech G as a native PC gaming brand has a far more natural and powerful fit with this audience and this game.

Why This Matters for Logitech G
  • Proof of concept, major brands have already invested at the $10–20K level in Deadlock esports
  • Community receptivity, Deadlock players embraced the Trolli brand activation enthusiastically
  • Creative space, in-game branded content is possible in a way no other title currently allows
  • Scene timing, this was before open beta. The audience is about to multiply significantly
Our Advantage Over DNS × Trolli
  • In-person LAN, physical presence, not just online
  • Guildhouse venue, professional production, 18K sq ft
  • Bay Area audience, tech-forward, PC gaming native
  • First US LAN ever, historic milestone branding

Working with Deadlock's community modding infrastructure, we can explore creating a custom Logitech G branded in-game asset, integrated into the game during the live broadcast.

  • Logitech G branding visible inside the game on every stream frame
